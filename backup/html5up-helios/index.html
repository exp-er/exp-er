What is Exp-er?

Exp-er as a community and a brand, is based on 4 pillars, Experiment, Experience, Expertise and Express.

Everything we do in life, starts from experimentation, it could be business, professional, personal or any other aspect of life, we start with an experiment.
Aim → Method → Result → Analysis → Conclusion.
Through these experiment, success or failures, we gain experience that build up our skill and knowledge.
We review our learnings and then re-do the experiments with the new knowledge, using the experience to facilitiate the growth.
With time, we gain expertise through these experiences, which for further growth, helps us design better experiments.
It helps us to efficiently gain insights from experiences, and finally, to be share our insights with people who are just starting the journey in that field.
The final pillar, is express, essential for our brand and as a community, to be able to express thoughts and ideas,
to be able to share and learn from each other. Be it story telling, presentations, pitching or just opening up.
